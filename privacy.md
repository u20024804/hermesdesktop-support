# HermesDesktop Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

HermesDesktop is a native macOS app that runs a local Hermes AI workspace in the macOS app sandbox. This policy describes the App Store build of HermesDesktop.

## Data Stored On Your Mac

HermesDesktop stores its app data in its macOS sandbox container. Depending on how you configure the app, this may include local workspace files, app settings, provider configuration, API keys, Telegram bot tokens, Telegram user IDs, logs, and local runtime state.

## Data Sent To Services You Configure

HermesDesktop does not operate a developer-hosted backend for app data. When you configure third-party services, the app may send the data needed to provide the requested feature to those services. Examples include:

- AI model providers such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or OpenRouter, when you configure a provider and send prompts or context for model responses.
- Telegram, when you choose to link a Telegram bot and use Telegram messaging.

Those services process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

## Telegram

Telegram linking is optional. If enabled, HermesDesktop may use your Telegram bot token, allowed Telegram user IDs, and Telegram message content to send messages to and from the configured Telegram bot. Non-Telegram messaging adapters are disabled in the App Store build.

## Tracking And Analytics

HermesDesktop does not include advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or developer-operated telemetry services. The developer does not sell personal data.

## Secrets

Do not share API keys, Telegram bot tokens, passwords, or other secrets in public support channels. Remove secrets from logs before sending them for support.
